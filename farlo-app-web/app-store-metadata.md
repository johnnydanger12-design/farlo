## Description (4000 chars max)

Farlo connects customers with local businesses — food trucks, coffee shops, restaurants, pop-ups, and more. Find who's open nearby, browse menus, place orders, and follow your favorites.

**For Customers**

• See open businesses on a live map — updated in real time
• Browse full menus and place orders directly in the app
• Follow your favorite spots and get announcements straight to your phone — no algorithm, no feed to scroll
• Request private event bookings for catering, corporate events, parties, and more
• Leave reviews and discover new places based on what's near you

**For Business Owners**

Get your business in front of local customers without building your own app.

• Go live on the map so nearby customers can find you instantly
• Accept online orders and get paid directly — Farlo never touches your funds
• Send announcements to your followers with one tap
• Manage employees and shift scheduling in one place
• Handle private event and catering requests with built-in messaging and payment tools
• Works for mobile businesses (food trucks, pop-ups) and fixed locations (cafés, restaurants, shops)

**Built for independence**

Farlo is for independent operators who want the tools big chains have — without the monthly software bills that eat into margins. One subscription, everything included.

14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
